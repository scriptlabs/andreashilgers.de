# Portfolio: Selected Projects

A selection of strategic projects and platforms that have significantly contributed to digital transformation over the last 20 years.

## 1. Firepire
**Category:** Web3 & AI News  
**Role:** Lead Architect & Developer  
**Tech:** NestJS, Next.js, Python, PostgreSQL, Redis, Terraform, GCP, Web3, Perplexity AI.

- High-performance, modular full-stack platform for crypto news and market data.
- Combines microservice orchestration with scalable frontend and automated data workers.
- AI-driven CMS for news with automated enrichment via Perplexity AI.
- Multi-Cloud deployment (GCP, AWS, Azure).

## 2. MemoryMesh AI
**Category:** AI Infrastructure  
**Role:** Co-founder & Lead Architect  
**Tech:** Next.js, NestJS, Turborepo, PostgreSQL, pgvector, Vercel AI SDK, Docker.

- "The AI that remembers you" – a platform for persistent, personalized AI interactions.
- Intelligent two-layer memory architecture (PostgreSQL + pgvector).
- Asynchronous compression pipeline to distill conversations into a personal knowledge graph.

## 3. Online Cargo Planner
**Category:** Full-Service Platform  
**Role:** Lead Full-Stack Developer  
**Tech:** Node.js, PHP, Laravel, React Native, Google Gemini, Google Cloud, AI.

- End-to-end logistics platform for rail freight services.
- Managed orders, documents, certificates, and compliance.
- Back-office suite for dispatching, accounting, team management, and HR.

## 4. HUNCHster
**Category:** Open Innovation SaaS

## 5. Computerbild Cloud
**Category:** Cloud Storage Platform  
**Role:** Lead Frontend & API Developer
